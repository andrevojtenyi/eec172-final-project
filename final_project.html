Dual Control Pong

EEC 172 Spring 2025 Final Project

A simple game of Pong played with two different controllers. Player 1 uses a CC3200's accelerometer to control their paddle by tilting the board, while Player 2 uses an IR remote to move their paddle with UP and DOWN buttons. After a player wins, they're prompted to enter a three-letter name, which is then used to update a leaderboard tracking the number of wins per player.
